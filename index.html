About Bosko Designs

Hey I'm Stephanie and I've been involved in various design industries for the past two decades. My experience is a combination of technical and creative. I started out in drafting and engineering. I gradually shifted into photography, web design, art and illustration. I've gravitated towards traditional media as a profession nowadays. But yes, I do work with digital media such as graphic tablets, vectors and software. That's one of the reasons I'm here. What am I offering? In a nutshell - stuff. Follow the octopus to see what I mean. Stuff like illustration work for comics, books, story-boarding. Pencils, inks, that sort of perlarva. (My favourite) Other stuff like branding / logo designs for your wee (or big) company. Shop front signs? Yup. (Heck, even a tattoo design if you want) All the shiny buttons and images that make a website pretty? I'm on it. (Maybe just a wee banner for your social media page). Sooo...erm...take a look around and see if I press any buttons for you. Fnar.
