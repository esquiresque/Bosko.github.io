PORTRAITS

A selection of private and commissioned portrait work. I work in pastel or pencil for work with pets and people. I find this media allows for a loose, expressive look as well as a more realistic profile when required. My personal preferences often follow a fusion of the two techniques. Papers are usually ingres-style or canson pastel papers.
